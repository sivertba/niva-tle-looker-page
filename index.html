Satellite Forecaster

This website contains a forecast of satellite passes for the next week. At the bottom of the site you can see the different satellites and the different locations that are used in the forecast. The forecast is generated using the pyorbital library. The forecast is generated for the next week and is updated every day. The cloud cover is retrieved from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute.
